GSAn

Welcome to GSAn

Gene Set Annnotation is a public web server for characterising gene lists of high-throughput genomics. GSAn has a simple user-friendly web interface with powerful visualisation in result. Actually, GSAn use only the Gene Ontology and the Gene Ontology Annotation and the information stored is updated every week. The aim of GSAn is provide a synthetic annotation for a given gene set.


About GSAn

GSAn is developed and maintained in France, at the University of Bordeaux, in collaboration with Laboratoire bordolaise en recherche informatic (LaBRI) and Institut de santé public et epidemiologie et développement (ISPED). The main development has been done during the PhD of Aaron Ayllon-Benitez over the supervision of Patricia Thébault and Fleur Mougin. The first version of GSAn became available in early 2018. GSAn tool is freely available through web application and various programatic access points.
